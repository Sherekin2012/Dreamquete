About Us

Welcome to Dreamquete Exotic Shorthair Cats! We are a small show breeder located in northwest Indiana dedicated to producing top quality International Winning Cats. Our cats are registered with both TICA & CFA.

Our Cats

All of our cats are part of the family. They play with the dogs and supervise all that goes on.

Our Kittens

Our kittens are raised with a lot of love and attention. People who have adopted our babies often comment on how super sweet and affectionate they are.

Winners

Our breeding program consistently produces cats of excellent type, health, and winning personality.
