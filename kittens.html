Our kittens

Our kittens are raised with a lot of love and attention. As soon a it is safe for them to do so, they are out and about with us, other cats, and our dogs.
People who have adopted our babies often comment on how super sweet and affectionate our kittens are. We think part of that is inherent in the Exotic breed, and we make sure that we develop those sweet dispositions with lots of socialization.

While the majority of our kittens remain with us or go to others looking for a baby to show, we do occasionally have kittens, or retired adults, looking for forever pet homes. Please contact us for more information about what we have available now.
